⚙️ FastText Algorithm (Simple Explanation)

fastText improves on Word2Vec by:

1. Subword modeling
Each word is broken into character n-grams
Example:
"where" → <wh, whe, her, ere, re>
Final word vector = sum of n-gram vectors

➡️ This allows handling unseen words and morphology

2. Shallow neural model
Similar to Word2Vec (CBOW / Skip-gram)
Uses:
Linear layer (no deep network)
Very fast training

3. Efficient classification
Uses:
Bag of words + n-grams
Hierarchical softmax (tree-based classification)
🧠 Why fastText is important
Works well on large-scale text
Handles rare & misspelled words
Much faster than deep models (sometimes seconds vs days)
